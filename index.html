Privacy Policy

Race to the Moon

Developer: eMilkyWay Studio
Effective date: July 19, 2026
Last updated: July 21, 2026

eMilkyWay Studio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when you download, install, access, or play Race to the Moon (the "Game"). It also explains the choices and rights that may be available to you.

This Policy applies to the Android version of the Game distributed through Google Play. The Windows version does not show advertising. By using the Android version, you acknowledge the practices described below. Where consent is required by applicable law, it is requested separately; this Policy is not consent.

1. Information handled by the Game

The Game does not require you to create an eMilkyWay Studio account. Unless a feature clearly states otherwise, we do not directly ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or financial information.

The Game may handle the following categories of information:

  • Local game data: settings, preferences, progress, scores, achievements, and similar gameplay information may be stored locally on your device. This local game data is not transmitted to eMilkyWay Studio.
  • Advertising and technical data: when you choose to view an optional rewarded ad, Google AdMob and Google may automatically process the data described in Section 2.
  • Communications: if you contact us for support, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message, and any diagnostic details or attachments you send.

2. Advertising through Google AdMob

The Android version of the Game may show only optional rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob. The Game does not show banner, interstitial, app-open, or mediated advertisements. Viewing a rewarded ad is never required to play the Game: you choose whether to start it after Game Over or Flight Complete. If an eligible rewarded ad is completed, you receive an in-game XP bonus that doubles the XP for that flight.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as:

  • IP address, which may be used to estimate general location;
  • game and advertisement interactions, such as app launches, taps, and video views;
  • diagnostic and performance information, such as crash, launch-time, hang-rate, or energy-usage data; and
  • device or account identifiers, which may include the Android Advertising ID, App Set ID, and other applicable identifiers.

This information may be used to serve and, where permitted, personalize the optional rewarded ad; measure advertising performance; limit repeated ads; and detect fraud, abuse, and security issues. Google and AdMob process information under their own privacy terms.

Depending on your region, the Game may ask for your advertising choices through Google's consent platform or another legally compliant consent mechanism. You may be able to choose personalized, non-personalized, or limited advertisements, depending on availability and applicable law. You can also reset or delete your Android Advertising ID, or adjust ad-personalization controls, in your device or Google account settings. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google — How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and review the Google Privacy Policy.

3. How information is used

Information is used to:

  • save settings and provide gameplay features;
  • display and, where permitted, personalize optional rewarded ads;
  • measure advertising performance and diagnose technical problems;
  • protect the Game and advertising services against fraud, abuse, and security threats;
  • respond to support requests and other communications; and
  • comply with applicable laws, enforce our terms, and establish or defend legal claims.

4. Legal bases for processing

Where laws such as the GDPR or UK GDPR apply, the legal basis depends on the information and purpose involved. We may process information based on your consent (for example, for certain personalized advertising), to comply with legal obligations, or for legitimate interests such as operating and securing the Game, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights. You may withdraw consent at any time through the available privacy controls.

5. How information is shared

Information may be shared with:

  • Google and AdMob, when you choose to view an optional rewarded ad, to provide advertising services;
  • Authorities or other parties for legal and safety reasons, where reasonably necessary to comply with law or legal process, protect rights and safety, investigate fraud, or enforce agreements; and

eMilkyWay Studio does not sell personal information for monetary consideration. Advertising providers may process information for personalized advertising where permitted and consented to. Certain privacy laws may define some advertising-related disclosures more broadly as a “sale,” “sharing,” or “targeted advertising”; where applicable, you may exercise the choices described in Sections 2 and 8.

6. Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until it is deleted by the Game, you clear the Game's storage, or you uninstall the Game. Support communications and records directly held by us are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, including support, security, dispute resolution, and legal obligations. Google determines retention periods for information it processes under its own policies.

7. Security and international processing

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Google may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used for international transfers in accordance with applicable law.

8. Your choices and privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information;
  • request restriction of or object to certain processing;
  • receive certain information in a portable format;
  • withdraw consent without affecting prior lawful processing;
  • opt out of certain personalized or targeted advertising; and
  • submit a complaint to your local data-protection authority.

You may manage Google account and advertising settings directly through Google and your Android device. You may delete local game data by using any available in-game reset option, clearing the Game's application data in Android settings, or uninstalling the Game. Because the Game does not provide an eMilkyWay Studio account, there is no separate studio account to delete.

To exercise a right concerning information directly controlled by eMilkyWay Studio, contact us using the method in Section 11. We may need information sufficient to verify and process your request. For information controlled by Google or another provider, please use that provider's privacy controls or contact channels.

9. Children's privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information directly to us, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.
