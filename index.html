eMilkyWay Studio ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, shared, and protected when you download, install, access, or play Race to the Moon (the "Game"). It also explains the choices and rights that may be available to you.

This Policy applies to the Android version of the Game distributed through Google Play. By using the Game, you acknowledge the practices described below. Where consent is required by applicable law, we will request it separately and will not rely solely on this Policy as consent.

1. Information handled by the Game

The Game does not require you to create an eMilkyWay Studio account. Unless a feature clearly states otherwise, we do not directly ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or financial information.

The Game may handle the following categories of information:

  • Local game data: settings, preferences, progress, scores, achievements, and similar gameplay information may be stored locally on your device. This local game data is not transmitted to eMilkyWay Studio.
  • Advertising and technical data: where advertising is enabled, Google AdMob and its advertising partners may automatically process the data described in Section 2.
  • Communications: if you contact us for support, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, message, and any diagnostic details or attachments you send.

2. Advertising through Google AdMob

Released versions of the Game may display banner, interstitial, and rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob. Rewarded advertisements are optional and provide an in-game benefit after you choose to view an eligible advertisement.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share information such as:

  • IP address, which may be used to estimate general location;
  • game and advertisement interactions, such as app launches, taps, and video views;
  • diagnostic and performance information, such as crash, launch-time, hang-rate, or energy-usage data; and
  • device or account identifiers, which may include the Android Advertising ID, App Set ID, and other applicable identifiers.

This information may be used for serving and personalizing advertisements, measuring advertising performance, analytics, limiting repeated advertisements, and detecting fraud, abuse, and security issues. AdMob and participating ad technology providers process information under their own privacy terms.

Depending on your region, the Game may ask for your advertising choices through Google's consent platform or another legally compliant consent mechanism. You may be able to choose personalized, non-personalized, or limited advertisements, depending on availability and applicable law. You can also reset or delete your Android Advertising ID, or adjust ad-personalization controls, in your device or Google account settings. Withdrawing consent does not affect processing that was lawful before withdrawal.

Learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at Google — How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services and review the Google Privacy Policy.

If we add a new service or feature that materially changes how personal information is handled, we will update this Policy and, where required, provide an in-game notice or request consent before the new processing begins.

We and our service providers may use information to:

Google and other service providers may process information in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used for international transfers in accordance with applicable law.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise a right concerning information directly controlled by eMilkyWay Studio, contact us using the method in Section 12. We may need information sufficient to verify and process your request. For information controlled by Google or another provider, please use that provider's privacy controls or contact channels.
